Adoption

We got Nollie about six years ago now. My parents found her online and then my dad, who may be somewhat crazy, drove all the way to Montana to pick her up. In the middle of winter.

Type

Nollie is half golden retreiver and half border collie. She has all the fluff of a golden and the energy and brains of a border collie.
