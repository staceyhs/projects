My Favorite Places

A few favorites from places I have visited.

Kyoto, Japan

There will be some more text here eventually, but essentially it's one of the most beautiful places on the planet. It's also so clean for a city of that size. Though I suppose Japan in general is outstanding for that category. I will try and find some photos later.

Aix En Provence, France

If it wasn't for the smoking, I would probably say France in general was one of my favorites. But unfortunately I just can't deal with the smoking in Paris.

The Metropolitan Art Museum, NY

I love the Egyptian artifacts they have in there. But in general probably one of the best museums that I've visited.

Studio Ghibli Museum, Japan

As magical as you would imagine it to be. If you're ever interested in going, you'll probably have to have a Japanese friend or a tour group buy tickets for you. Buying tickets is ridiculously competitive during the normal tourist season. They sell out within minutes of going on sale -- and they only go on sale once a month for the following month! If you're interested in learning more, you can check out here from their official site, though I would also recommend checking out a few blogs/how to guides as well. It's a complicated process sometimes.
